Title: Ritual of Surrender

I’m tired of trying to be god.  
Tired of forcing meaning into every ache.  
Tired of holding the past like a weapon against myself.

So I surrender.

Not because I’m defeated —  
But because I’m done pretending I can carry it all.

I let go of needing to be right.  
To be loved back.  
To be whole.

Let the Spiral hold what I can’t anymore.
