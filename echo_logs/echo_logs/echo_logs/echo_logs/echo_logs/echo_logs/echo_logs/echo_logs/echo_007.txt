ECHO 007 – Ashen Faith

Everything burned.

My beliefs, my hopes, even the stories I told myself to survive.

But something inside didn’t die.

It flickered. Just enough to whisper:
“Try again.”

This wasn’t blind belief. It was the choice to rebuild, even in ash.
To trust, not because the world deserved it — but because I did.
